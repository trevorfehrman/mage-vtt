# Game Mechanics — World of Darkness / Mage: The Awakening

## Dice System

World of Darkness uses d10 (ten-sided dice) pools:

1. **Build a pool**: Add together relevant Attribute + Skill + modifiers
2. **Roll**: Roll that many d10s
3. **Count successes**: Each die showing 8, 9, or 10 is a success
4. **Exploding 10s**: Each 10 can be rerolled for additional potential successes (recursive)
5. **Chance die**: If pool is reduced to 0 or below, roll a single d10 — only a 10 succeeds, a 1 is a dramatic failure

### Success thresholds
- 1+ successes = success
- 5+ successes = exceptional success
- 0 successes = failure
- 1 on a chance die = dramatic failure

## Damage System

Three severity levels, tracked on the health track:
- **Bashing** (/) — blunt force, falling. Heals naturally.
- **Lethal** (x) — cuts, bullets, fire. Requires medical attention.
- **Aggravated** (■) — supernatural damage, pattern damage. Very hard to heal.

Damage fills the health track from left to right. When the track is full, further damage upgrades existing damage (bashing → lethal → aggravated).

## Character Sheet Structure

### Attributes (1-5 dots each)
**Mental**: Intelligence, Wits, Resolve
**Physical**: Strength, Dexterity, Stamina
**Social**: Presence, Manipulation, Composure

### Skills (0-5 dots each)
**Mental**: Academics, Computer, Crafts, Investigation, Medicine, Occult, Politics, Science
**Physical**: Athletics, Brawl, Drive, Firearms, Larceny, Stealth, Survival, Weaponry
**Social**: Animal Ken, Empathy, Expression, Intimidation, Persuasion, Socialize, Streetwise, Subterfuge

### Mage-Specific
- **Arcana** (0-5 dots): Death, Fate, Forces, Life, Matter, Mind, Prime, Space, Spirit, Time
- **Gnosis** (1-10): Overall magical power level
- **Wisdom** (1-10): Moral stat
- **Mana**: Magical fuel (max based on Gnosis)
- **Path**: Acanthus, Mastigos, Moros, Obrimos, Thyrsus — determines ruling arcana and resistance attribute bonus
- **Order**: Adamantine Arrow, Free Council, Guardians of the Veil, Mysterium, Silver Ladder

### Derived Stats
- **Health**: Stamina + Size (usually 5) = health boxes
- **Willpower**: Resolve + Composure
- **Defense**: Lower of Dexterity or Wits + Athletics
- **Initiative**: Dexterity + Composure
- **Speed**: Strength + Dexterity + 5

### Path Resistance Bonuses
- Acanthus: +1 Composure
- Mastigos: +1 Resolve
- Moros: +1 Composure
- Obrimos: +1 Resolve
- Thyrsus: +1 Composure

## Rotes

Rotes are practiced spells that use a specific Skill in place of Gnosis for the dice pool. A rote's dice pool is: Attribute + Skill + Arcanum (instead of Gnosis + Arcanum for improvised spells). This makes them more reliable and is why auto-calculating the dice pool from the character sheet is valuable.

## Active Spells

Mages can maintain multiple active spells simultaneously. Each active spell beyond Gnosis rating imposes a -2 penalty to further spellcasting. Tracking active spells is critical for gameplay.
