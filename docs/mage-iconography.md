# Mage: The Awakening — Visual Iconography Reference

Research asset for the `visual-identity` ticket (`docs/decision-map.md`). Compiled
for drawing **original** clean line-glyphs — motif + geometry, not tracing. The
canonical Path/Order runes and Kaluta-style illuminated emblems are copyrighted
line art; abstract the *motif* (spiral, fork, comma-dot, bracket, ladder,
gear-wheel, pentacle) into fresh geometry.

## The unifying grammar (most important finding)

The canonical Mage glyph is a **single confident calligraphic stroke**:
thick-to-thin, an **angular barbed point** at one terminal, an **inward
spiral/scroll curl** at the other, often one **solid punctuating dot** (a
"bindi"). Arabic/Tibetan calligraphy fused with alchemical sigils. This is High
Speech — a rune alphabet that resembles many earthly scripts but matches none,
"born whole, never pictographic."

> This is **calligraphic-runic**, not Euclidean-geometric. A cube / eight-point
> star / hourglass reads as generic sacred-geometry; a barbed spiral-terminal
> brush-stroke reads as *Mage specifically*. (See the fork noted in
> `visual-identity.md`.)

## The ten Arcana — one gesture each

| Arcanum | Canonical geometry | One-gesture motif |
|---|---|---|
| **Death** | Angular hook sweeping into a large inward **nautilus spiral**; downward barb. | A spiral that *closes inward* — draining whirlpool, entropy. |
| **Fate** | Open **U-cup / wishbone**; right horn extends into a **spiral tail**. | A forking path that loops back — bifurcation, the thread. |
| **Forces** | Bold **tomoe (comma)** wrapping a **solid filled dot**; barb on top. | A swirl orbiting a nucleus — the storm's eye. |
| **Life** | Horizontal **wing/horns** sweeping out both ways; spiral coil + dot center. | Horned seed sprouting bilaterally from a curled center. |
| **Matter** | Heavy **P/Q**: rounded bowl on a stem, flame-barb top, **crossbar** on stem. | A load-bearing stem with a structural crossbar — solidity. |
| **Mind** | Slender upright: vertical stroke, small **loop** mid-height, hook + barb. | An upright reed with a single thought-loop on a spine. |
| **Prime** | **Ankh / T-staff**: wide top bar over a stem ending in a **teardrop loop foot**. | Axis-mundi / key — staff crowned by a bar, footed by a loop. |
| **Space** | Angular **bracket/fold**: top-left curl, top bar, right shoulder, long tail. | A folded bracket tying two distant ends together. |
| **Spirit** | Bold angular **"3" / double-curve** (mirror-gateway) + a **solid dot** beside it. | A threshold/mirror with an orbiting soul-mote. |
| **Time** | Twin **peaks (M)** falling down a stem into a **spiral scroll** ("9" tail). | A stream falling from peaks into a coil — recurrence. |

**Subtle vs Gross (natural family grouping):** Subtle (unseen) = Death, Fate,
Mind, Prime, Spirit. Gross (physical) = Forces, Life, Matter, Space, Time.

**Realm pairing:** Aether → Prime + Forces · Arcadia → Fate + Time ·
Pandemonium → Mind + Space · Primal Wild → Spirit + Life · Stygia → Death + Matter.

| **Mastigos** — Iron Gauntlet / Pandemonium | Angular jagged "Z/2", barbed serifs, hooked tail. | Horned goetic figure, lion, thorn-crown. | Chain, gauntlet, jagged bolt; the whip (*mastix*). |

## The five Pentacle Orders

All four Diamond Orders descend from the **Dragon** (Draco) of Atlantis
(*"Draconis"* body-part names). The Free Council is the modern, non-Atlantean fifth.

| Order (Draconic) | Simple sigil |
|---|---|
| **Adamantine Arrow** (*Ungula* — Claw) | A sheaf of arrows crossed in a radiating **asterisk**, spiral at foot. |
| **Guardians of the Veil** (*Visus* — Eye) | Veiled **mask** (bar-slit eyes) with a **key-dagger** driven into the crown. |
| **Mysterium** (*Alae* — Wings) | An **open book bearing a keyhole** (a lock in the pages) / a winged keyhole. |
| **Silver Ladder** (*Vox* — Voice) | An **ascending ladder** with scrolled rungs, flanked by two orbs. |
| **Free Council** (modern) | A **gear-ringed concentric wheel** with a satellite orb — the assembly-wheel. |

*(Antagonist Seers of the Throne: a winged throne + radiant all-seeing eye +
lightning — imperial, top-down. Avoid this register for the protagonist UI.)*

## The Pentacle & sacred geometry

- The **Pentacle** = the five-Order alliance **and** the five-pointed star whose
  points are the five Supernal Realms. Diamond Orders ×4 + Free Council = the
  five working points.
- **Master layout device (most on-brand):** an upright **pentagram inscribed in
  a circle**, each vertex a Path/Realm glyph, each edge a relationship, the
  natural **inner pentagon** as a content well for a central mark (Prime / the
  character). Native to the setting, not decorative.
- **Atlantean aesthetic = high magic, not folk magic:** Platonic geometry,
  celestial/astronomical diagrams (orreries, star-charts, zodiac wheels),
  alchemical plates (retorts, planetary metals), temple/pillar architecture.
  Everything reads as *diagram / blueprint of reality*.
- **High Speech** has three registers: **Classical** (pure runes),
  **Illuminated** (pictograms), **Palimpsest** (runes overlapped into complex
  geometric sigils — good for seals/watermarks). Mage Sight = reality dissolving
  into shimmering interwoven strands that curl like strung-together runes.

## Art direction — what makes it *Atlantean high magic*

- **Grimoire, not gravestone** — a hand-illustrated sorcerer's grimoire /
  illuminated diary. Ink-on-vellum line art, not photo-grit.
- **Palette:** parchment/ivory grounds, sepia + iron-gall ink, **precious-metal
  accents** — silver/lunargent (Acanthus), gold/aether (Obrimos), lead/pewter
  (Moros). Jewel-tone Supernal light against a muted fallen world. **Verdigris
  #6fae97 sits naturally as an oxidized-copper "aged alchemical instrument"
  accent.**
- **Genre contrast:** Vampire = gothic blood-and-shadow (avoid heavy gothic
  ornament); Werewolf = primal claw/fetish (only Thyrsus touches this); **Mage =
  celestial + diagrammatic — intellectual awe, not dread.** Unveiling the
  blueprint of reality; wonder edged with hubris.
- **Texture cues:** engraving hatch-lines, compass-and-ruler construction marks,
  concentric measured circles, faint gridded "ley" lines. Light as **emanation**
  (thin radiating strokes), never glow-bloom.

## Motifs to exploit for a restrained UI

1. **The single calligraphic stroke** — every icon one brush-gesture (barbed
   point + inward spiral). One family, ten+ marks, legible at 16px.
2. **The punctuating dot (bindi)** — a lone solid dot as accent / active-state /
   Mana pip; the connective tissue across Forces, Spirit, Thyrsus, Obrimos.
3. **The pentacle-in-circle as master layout** — five vertices, inner pentagon
   content well, edges as relationships.
4. **Concentric measured circles / orrery** — nested rings, tick-marks, a
   satellite orb (Free Council) for radial menus, dials, progress.
5. **Metal-coded families** — silver / gold / lead / verdigris to distinguish
   Realms, Orders, or Arcana without hue-noise.
6. **Construction-line texture** — faint compass arcs, ruler grids, ley-lines
   → "blueprint of reality," not gothic grime.
7. **Emanation lighting** — accents as thin radiating strokes, never neon bloom.
8. **Palimpsest overlap** — two clean runes overlapped into one composite sigil
   for seals / watermarks / loading. Canonical, and distinctive without being IP.
